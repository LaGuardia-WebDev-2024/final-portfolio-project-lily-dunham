About Me

One of my favorite things to do is embroidery. I had an embroidery class in middle school and after that I started to do it all the time for fun. I've made keychains and camera straps and guitar straps and pencil cases and I've even embroidered peoples names or initials onto jean jackets or sweatpants for them.

Another thing that I like to do in my free time is write plays. I wrote a play in sophmore year called 'in the cosmos' and it was a semi-finalist at the Blank Theatre and it was selected as one of the winners at the O'Neill and I was invited to participate in their Young Playwrights Festival where I workshopped the play and heard it read and enjoyed a three-day-intensive where I took thought-provoking classes and seminars before returning home to New York City. Last summer I took a two-week playwrighting class with Crystal Skillman at TDF where I wrote and developed my two short plays Bang! and Tots. I hope to continue writing plays over this summer.

Another thing that I love to do is play and write music. I've been playing the acoustic guitar for 8 years and I took up the ukulele and electric guitar around the same time. I took piano lessons for a number of years and I've been playing the bass guitar for the last 3. I can read sheet music (not VERY well but whateva) but more often than not I perfer to play a song on my phone and then play it by ear.
